Our story!

We saw the need for authentic Caribbean cuisine in a niche marketplace & we decided to fill it. As Jamaican migrants who were born and raised on the island, we came with the knowledge of the taste, spices, and original techniques that makes Jamaican food world renowned.

We served our first customer on August 17, 2013 at an out-door flea in Queens, New York. This was the first day that we took our cuisine to the public. We were excited, yet nervous about the potential outcome; but to our elation, our food went over well and the demand has been growing ever since. Since then we have grown dramatically. We have opened three (3) locations in only two (2) years and now researching a fourth (4th) location. This stands as testament to our food quality and cuisine.

​

Our Concept!

We offer a Fast Casual service which gives our customers an upfront view of our cuisine and offers the option of choice. Choose one, two or even three entrees with your choice of one or two starch or vegetable sides. Our wish is to have you​ join us on our journey & Let us show you ... "Real Caribbean Food" ... Its not just a brand, its a Lifestyle.

​

David Wisdom - C.E.O & Founder
